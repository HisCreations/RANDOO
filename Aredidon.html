Aredidon Video

Our teacher, Aredidon, is basically a giant dinosaur who stomps into the classroom like she just escaped a prehistoric zoo. When she starts explaining lessons, she suddenly transforms into a fire-breathing dragon—except instead of burning villages, she burns us with surprise quizzes. Her footsteps are so loud we half expect the ceiling to shake like a T-Rex just discovered homework. Honestly, if dinosaurs and dragons ever went back to school, Aredidon would be their principal and we’d all still be late to class. 🦖🐉😆
